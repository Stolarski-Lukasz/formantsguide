Welcome to Formants Guide, a site that assists you in estimating formant values for vowel articulation. Once you have measured formants in acoustic analysis software such as Praat, you can compare the measurements with the values in different regions of the IPA Vowel Diagram. The values obtained on this site are averages for the sample of phoneticians you have chosen in the panel "Phonetician Group".
